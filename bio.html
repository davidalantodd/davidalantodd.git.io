biography

Hello! My name is David (he/him) and I'm a Software Engineering Coach at Multiverse. I was born and raised in Ellicott City, MD and I currently live in Boston, MA.

I have always had an interest in the intersection of people and technology. I attended the University of Maryland and earned a dual degree in Computer Science and Theatre. After college, I pursued a master's degree at the University of Georgia to become a higher education administrator. I spent several years creating learning experiences, developing collaborative teams, and implementing new software to streamline processes. In the next phase of my career, I knew I wanted to combine my knowledge of computer science with my experiences in education.

I haven't always felt a sense of belonging in the tech field. As someone who is queer and multiracial with immigrant parents (from the Cayman Islands and the UK), I seek out spaces where authenticity, equity, and social justice are valued.
